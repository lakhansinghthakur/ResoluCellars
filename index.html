Cellar Club

Award winning wine-makers, Scott Nelson and his son, Cameron, have created Résolu Cellars to produce single varietal wines and unique blends that stand out for their overall quality and value, vintage after vintage. Combining considerable experience, industry recognition, hard work, and proven expertise, Résolu Cellars delivers a collection of ultra-premium NW wines that suit a wide spectrum of wine-occasions ranging from the cocktail hour to fancy meals to celebrations.

Résolu represents the excellence to which Scott and Cameron have devoted themselves. Grape selection is based on careful study of which varieties thrive in the Northwest, and which growers bring a winning partnership.

Cellar Club

Membership Matters! Join the family of wine appreciators and be an integral part of growing Résolu Cellars. We value our club members as it is the best way to support our growing winery and have a direct connection to the winemakers and their wines.It is our desire to build lasting friendships and create memorable winery and wine experiences through a shared love of finely crafted wine. Through our two “build your own” wine clubs, members have priority access to Résolu Cellars’ wines and winemakers.

We've grown our club community through our twice yearly “Pick-up Events” where we can spoil our members, meet their friends and show off the newly released wines. 2020 is the year of the new for us finding innovative ways to include wine in your story!
